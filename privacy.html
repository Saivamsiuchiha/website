Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025  ·  chillreach.ai

chillreach.ai is a cold email infrastructure and lead generation service run by Malyakula Sai Vamsi. We take your privacy seriously. This page explains what data we collect, why, and how we handle it.

1. No cookies. No tracking.

We do not use cookies on this website. We do not run analytics, retargeting pixels, or any third-party tracking scripts. When you visit chillreach.ai, no data is stored in your browser and no tracking identifiers are created.

2. What we collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide to us:

  • Booking data — when you book a call via cal.com, your name, email, and any notes you submit are collected by cal.com under their own privacy policy. We receive this information to prepare for and follow up on the call.
  • Email correspondence — if you email us directly at sai@chillreach.org, we store that communication to respond and follow up.
  • WhatsApp messages — if you contact us via WhatsApp, messages are stored within WhatsApp's platform under their privacy policy.

3. How we use your data

We use your information solely to:

  • Respond to enquiries and prepare for strategy calls
  • Deliver and manage services for active clients
  • Send you campaign reports and updates relevant to your account

We do not use your data for marketing purposes without your consent. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Third-party services we use

To operate our business, we use the following services that may process your data:

  • cal.com — booking and scheduling
  • Google Workspace — email and document management
  • Instantly.ai / Smartlead — campaign delivery (for active clients only)

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We only share the minimum data necessary to deliver our services.

5. Data retention

We retain client data for the duration of the engagement and up to 12 months after it ends, unless you request deletion sooner. Enquiry data from non-clients is deleted within 90 days if no engagement begins.

6. Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To make a request, email us at sai@chillreach.org and we will respond within 7 business days.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, reach out directly:

  • Email: sai@chillreach.org
  • Phone: +91 93922 74091

This policy applies to the chillreach.ai website and services. It may be updated periodically — the date at the top reflects the latest revision.
